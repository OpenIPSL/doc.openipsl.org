OpenIPSL.Electrical.ThreePhase.Branches

Multi-phase and hybrid positive-sequence/three-phase lines and transformers

Information

This package contains three-phase models for branches, that is, three-phase transformers and lines. It also contains hybrid positive-sequence/three-phase models for transformers and power lines.
