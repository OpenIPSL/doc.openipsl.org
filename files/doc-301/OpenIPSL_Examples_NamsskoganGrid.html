OpenIPSL.Examples.NamsskoganGrid.Grid_Model

Namsskogan distribution network modeled in positive sequence equivalent

Information

This example is composed by the 33-bus 12-machine system representing a distribution-level subsystem of Norway.

The system undergoes a three-phase fault on Bus 26 at 1s, lasting for 20ms. Simulate the system for 10 seconds.

Variables of interest are the voltages in many buses. For example:

  • B3.v
  • B11.v
  • B26.v
